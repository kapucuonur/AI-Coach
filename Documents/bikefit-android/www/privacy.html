Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Welcome to BikeFit AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Camera and Video Data

Our application requires access to your device's camera to perform real-time biomechanical analysis. We do not record, store, or share your live video feed or photos without your explicit consent. The video frames are processed instantaneously by our AI models to calculate joint angles and then immediately discarded. No video footage is saved permanently to our cloud servers without your explicit manual upload.

2. User Data

We do not collect or sell personally identifiable information (PII) to third parties. Any biomechanical data, angles, or measurements generated during your session are processed temporarily for the purpose of generating your BikeFit report.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use standard third-party tools to improve the application. These tools do not have access to your camera feed or personal measurements.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@coachonurai.com.
